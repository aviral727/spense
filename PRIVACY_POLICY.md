# Privacy Policy for Spense

**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 12, 2026

Spense ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Spense mobile application (the "App").

## 1. 100% On-Device Processing

Spense is built on a strict **privacy-first** philosophy. **We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your personal or financial data on external servers.**

All operations — including transaction entry, categorization, and budget calculations — are performed strictly **on your device**. The App does not require an internet connection to function, and no cloud backups or sync services are used.

## 2. Permissions We Request

Spense requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

- **INTERNET:** Used solely for loading fonts and optional framework resources at startup. No user data is transmitted.
- **VIBRATE:** Used to provide haptic feedback for notifications.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE:** Used to support reliable notification delivery when the App is backgrounded.
- **NOTIFICATIONS:** Used to alert you about new transactions you record and budget threshold warnings.

Spense does **not** request access to your SMS messages, call logs, contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

## 3. Data You Enter

All transaction records, budget configurations, and categories are entered manually by you and stored locally in an SQLite database on your device. This data is:

- **Never uploaded** to any server
- **Never shared** with any third party
- **Permanently deleted** if you uninstall the App or clear its data (no external backup exists unless you back up your device manually)

## 4. Third-Party Services

Spense does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services that collect user data. The App is entirely self-contained.

## 5. Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of your financial information relies entirely on the security of your device. We recommend using a strong screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Spense does not collect any personally identifiable information from any user, including children. As the App operates entirely offline and collects no data, it poses no privacy risks to minors.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes through app updates on the Google Play Store or via the GitHub repository where the App is hosted.
